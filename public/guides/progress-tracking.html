Track Your Recall Accuracy & Progress

Learn how to monitor and improve your BLD performance over time

Why Track Your Progress?

Tracking your progress is essential for systematic improvement in blindfold cubing. By monitoring your performance, you can:

  • Identify Weaknesses: Find which drills or letter pairs need more practice
  • Measure Improvement: See your progress over time and stay motivated
  • Optimize Training: Focus your practice on areas that need the most work
  • Set Goals: Establish realistic targets and track your progress toward them
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Use statistics to guide your training strategy

Improving Recall Accuracy

Recall accuracy is one of the most important metrics for BLD success. Here's how to improve it:

Techniques for Better Recall:
  • Increase Vividness: More vivid images are easier to recall
  • Improve Story Fluency: Better story flow makes sequences easier to remember
  • Practice Weak Pairs: Identify and strengthen pairs with low recall accuracy
  • Reduce Cognitive Load: Simplify stories and imagery for easier recall
  • Practice Recall: Regularly test your recall to strengthen memory
Using Recall Accuracy Data:
  • Review sessions with low accuracy to identify patterns
  • Compare accuracy across different drill types
  • Identify which letter pairs are most problematic
  • Track accuracy improvements as you practice
  • Set accuracy goals and work toward them

Setting Goals and Benchmarks

Use progress tracking to set realistic goals and measure your improvement:

Beginner Goals:
  • Recall Accuracy: 70%+
  • Visualization Speed: 2-3 seconds per pair
  • Vividness: Average of 3+ (Clear)
  • Consistency: Practice 3+ times per week
Intermediate Goals:
  • Recall Accuracy: 85%+
  • Visualization Speed: 1-2 seconds per pair
  • Vividness: Average of 4+ (Vivid)
  • Flow: Average of 2+ (Smooth) for chain drills
Advanced Goals:
  • Recall Accuracy: 95%+
  • Visualization Speed: Under 1 second per pair
  • Vividness: Average of 4.5+ (Crystal)
  • Flow: Average of 2.5+ (Seamless) for chain drills
