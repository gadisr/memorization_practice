Interactive Cube with Letters

This interactive cube demonstrates the lettering system used in blindfold cubing. Each sticker position on the cube is assigned a letter according to the Speffz notation system.

Understanding the Lettering System

  • Edges use lowercase letters (a-x) - 24 edge stickers total
  • Corners use uppercase letters (A-X) - 24 corner stickers total
  • The buffer position for edges is b* (UR position)
  • The buffer position for corners is A* (UBL position)
  • Each piece has a specific letter assigned to help you track and memorize piece movements during blindfold solving
